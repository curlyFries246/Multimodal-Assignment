EL PASO NEEDS YOUR HELP

El Paso has a stagering 20.1% poverty rate and a population of 974,000 people. That means that 195,774 people are financially insecure. That number doesnt take into account
all the migrants crossing the border every single day. Between October 1, 2022 and December 31, 2022 El Paso sector border patrol reported 162,603 encounters.
Migrants come to El Paso with nothing, they are people who are looking for a better life for themselves and their famalies. Thankfully there exists organizations like El Pasoans Fighting Hunger
that provide food to the most vulnerable in our community.
