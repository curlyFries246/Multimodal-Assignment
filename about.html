WHAT IS EL PASOANS FIGHTING HUNGER

El Pasoans Fighting Hunger (EPHS) is an organization located in El Paso whos objective is to combat the hunger crisis in the bordrlands by supplying local food pantries.
EPFH is able to opperate as a non profit because of the help of volunteers and donations from local buisnesses. The service EPFH provides helps feed over 160,000 food insecure people.
EPFHs cause is one that is honest, noble, and worth getting behing. Without their contribution 1/3 of El Pasos population would lose a life line.
