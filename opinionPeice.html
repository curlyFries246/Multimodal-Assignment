WHY SHOULD YOU HELP

El Paso already has a abnormally high poverty rate for a metropolitan city but due to Tittle 42 ending on March 11, 2023 the border has had a major influx of migrants crossing over
from South America looking for asylum and because of El Paso's proximity to the border the number of migrants coming into El Paso has overwhelmed local shelters and community fascilitties.
As a result, many migrants find themselves having to sleep on the streets and resort to panhandling to support themselves. All the more reason to contribute to El Pasoans Fighting Hunger
and make a difference in our community.
